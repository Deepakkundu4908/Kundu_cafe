# 🖼️ Product Images & Description Modal - User Guide

## Overview
The Kundu Cafe application now features **clickable product images** that display detailed information and descriptions in an elegant modal popup!

---

## 🎯 How to Use

### **On Products Page** (`/products`)

#### 1. **View All Products**
   - Browse through our complete product catalog
   - See product names, categories, prices, and stock info
   - Images are displayed for each product

#### 2. **Click Product Image**
   - **Click the product image** to open a detailed modal
   - Modal shows:
     - Large product image
     - Complete product name
     - Category and subcategory
     - Dietary information (🌱 Veg / 🍗 Non-Veg)
     - Preparation time
     - **Full product description**
     - Current price
     - Stock availability
     - Quantity selector
     - Add to Cart button

#### 3. **Quick Add to Cart**
   - Use the **"Add to Cart" button** directly on the product card
   - Or adjust quantity in the modal and click "Add to Cart"

---

### **On Menu Browsing Page** (`/products/menu`)

#### 1. **Filter by Category**
   - 🌅 Breakfast
   - 🍽️ Lunch
   - 🍿 Snacks
   - ☕ Drinks

#### 2. **Filter by Dietary Preference**
   - All Items
   - 🌱 Vegetarian
   - 🍗 Non-Vegetarian

#### 3. **Click Product Image**
   - Opens modal with full details
   - Same information as products page
   - Updated to show filtered results

#### 4. **Click Product Name**
   - Alternative way to open modal
   - Shows same detailed information

---

### **On Product Detail Page**

#### 1. **View Large Image**
   - Product image displays prominently
   - Shows "Click to expand" hint

#### 2. **Expand Image**
   - Click the product image
   - Opens full-size expandable modal
   - View product image in detail
   - Click outside or press Escape to close

#### 3. **View Details & Purchase**
   - Product information on the right side
   - Add to cart with custom quantity
   - View stock availability

---

## 📱 Modal Features

### **Opening the Modal**
- ✅ Click product image
- ✅ Click product name (menu page only)
- ✅ Opens with smooth animation

### **Using the Modal**
1. **View Details**
   - Read complete product description
   - See all product information
   - Check price and stock

2. **Add to Cart**
   - Select quantity using the input field
   - Click "Add to Cart" button
   - Product added to your cart

3. **Close Modal**
   - Click the **X button** in top-right
   - Click outside the modal
   - Press **Escape key** on keyboard
   - Body automatically scrolls back to normal

## 💡 Tips & Tricks

### **Quick Navigation**
1. Browser back button closes modal
2. Multiple products can be added without leaving modal area
3. Use Escape key for quick close

### **Information You Can Find**
- **Description:** Full product details (uses, ingredients hints, etc.)
- **Dietary:** Vegetarian or Non-Vegetarian (🌱 or 🍗)
- **Prep Time:** How long to prepare (in minutes)
- **Price:** Current pricing
- **Stock:** How many items available (or "Out of Stock" message)
- **Category:** Product category (Breakfast, Lunch, Snacks, Drinks)

### **Customizing Your Order**
- Adjust quantity spinner in modal before adding to cart
- Maximum quantity limited by available stock
- Your quantity preference carries to the cart

## ❓ FAQ

**Q: Can I add to cart from the modal?**
A: Yes! Simply select quantity and click "Add to Cart" in the modal.

**Q: How do I close the modal?**
A: Multiple ways:
- Click the X button in top-right
- Click outside the modal
- Press Escape key on keyboard

**Q: Can I expand the product image?**
A: On the product detail page, clicking the image opens an expandable modal. On product/menu pages, the modal itself shows a large image.

**Q: Does the modal save my quantity?**
A: The quantity carries to your cart when you click "Add to Cart". After closing, it resets for the next product.

**Q: Is the modal mobile-friendly?**
A: Yes! The modal is fully responsive and works on all device sizes.

**Q: Can I see all product details without the modal?**
A: Yes, visit the product detail page for a traditional view with all information displayed.
